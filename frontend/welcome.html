Talarias (Template Launcher)

Templates folder

Choose the folder where your templates will live.

Each first-level subfolder inside that folder will appear in the dropdown at the bottom of the editor.

Only one level is shown in that dropdown. If one of those folders contains another subfolder, that second level will not appear there.

How it works

  • Put your .md or .txt templates inside the selected folder.
  • Start typing to search. Use Arrow keys to navigate and Enter to open a template.
  • You can edit the text before copying.
  • Press Escape to go back to the search input.

Power features

  • Alias Assign a short keyword to any template for faster search.
  • Shortcut Bind a keyboard shortcut to copy a template directly to clipboard without opening the app.
  • Folder filter Use the dropdown at the bottom of the editor to load templates from a specific first-level subfolder.

Global shortcut to bring the app to front

Press a key combination to change it, or leave it as is.
